Could A.I. Redeem the Israel-Hamas War?

Artificial Intelligence has been investigated in combat, especially in relation to the Israel-Hamas conflict. There are now worries of a broader Middle East crisis following Hamas's recent surprise strike. Since the end of the Cold War, artificial intelligence (AI) has been utilized in military operations. Israel employs AI in airstrikes, logistics, and the surveillance of unauthorized weapon transfers. The application of AI in the battle is still controversial, though, with some claiming it may be a sign of a bug or the need for improved data sharing. Human interaction and the potential for phony photos are drawbacks. By using AI, Hamas could be able to win over followers in Lebanon, Syria, or Iran, but it might have trouble gaining access to cutting-edge technology.
